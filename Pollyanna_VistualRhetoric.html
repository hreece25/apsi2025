Visual Rhetoric (Pollyanna the Hamster)

This activity provides a fun, low-risk way for students to think critically and peform informal rhetorical analysis without the pressure of a timed essay.

  • 1 - Contextualize the rhetorical situation in the graphic.
  • 2 - Interpret the message that the cartoonist is attempting to convey.
  • 3 - Describe any philosophical tensions that may be present in the graphic.

  • Your paragraphs must be written in complete sentences, with a thesis in the first paragraph and a topic sentence in the 2nd.
